        LiuGong 8128H Rocks in the Iron Mine in Mongolia

        In October, with the snow covering the mountains, the temperature drops to - 5 ℃ in Mongolia. However, LiuGong’s 8128H still works closely with a 888 in the pit, busy shoveling iron ore. According to the pit manager, they chose LiuGong wheel loaders from the very beginning of their business and now they own nearly 30 LiuGong machines, including loaders, excavators, rollers, graders, cranes among which there are more than 10 large-tonnage machines like the 8128H and the 888. In the past decades, LiuGong has won customers’ trust with both qualified products and dedciated services.

        Wuzhou Ceramic Industrial Park

        In the city of Wuzhou in Guangxi Province, a transformation is taking place. The area is aiming to become the "New Ceramic Capital of Southern China”. To accomplish this, a new industrial park is being developed, the Guangxi Wuzhou Ceramic Industrial Park. And of course, as with all construction projects of this size, large scale construction equipment is needed, and the equipment has to be productive and efficient, as meeting construction deadlines is paramount. As a result, over 20 large machines are working in the industrial park to ensure the project can be completed on time.
